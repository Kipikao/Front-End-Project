The company's mission is to offer its customers a 360-degree service

Offering innovative solutions in the field of design and activity management in order to optimize the costs

The knowledge acquired over the years by the specialists present in U.P.M. PROJECT S.r.l., made it possible to structure the company into two specific and complementary departments if necessary:

  • The first refers to design in which there are: methodologists, process analysts, designers.

  • The second concerns the analysis and control of production processes for orders assigned to external suppliers where there are Project Managers experts in the field to check the work progress of all production phases related to the construction of molds and machines.

This formula allows the customer to avoid using their staff for travel, optimizing order costs and maintaining the workforce in the Company.
